Questions, answered.

Is it really private?

Yes — structurally. Moonshot is a static page with no backend. Images are read into your browser’s memory, composited on a local canvas, and saved from there. Nothing is transmitted, logged, or stored remotely. You can verify this in your browser’s network tab: after the page loads, no requests carry your image anywhere.

What does the free version include?

The full editor — every frame, every canvas size, captions, grain, 2× export. Free exports carry a small, tasteful “Made with Moonshot” mark in the corner. Pro removes it, adds 12 premium backdrops and 3× export.

Is Pro really a one-time purchase?

Yes. Pay once — with ETH directly in the app (verified on-chain, unlocks instantly), or by card once the card checkout opens — and Pro is yours forever, future updates included.

Which browsers work?

Any modern browser — Chrome, Edge, Safari 16+, Firefox. Clipboard-copy works best in Chrome and Safari; classic file export works everywhere.

Can I use exports commercially?

Yes — anything you make with Moonshot is yours, free tier included.
